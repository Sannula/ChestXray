AI diagnosis powered by Cali (POC)

THIS IS AN ALPHA TEST NOT READY FOR PUBLISH. This is a web based AI that detects CT\X-ray chest section to predict diagnosis and explain its prediction. The patient data will be encrypted for its confidentiality and saved in your PC. Why did Samer do this POC:

  1. Show the potential of Cali AI Division and where It's going.
  2. To help us as AI Team to bring more brightful ideas to the table.
  3. Present ideas for our cloud services.
  4. This solution can classify the prioroty level of the patients in the ER

The POC contains three main components:
Heatmap System: In order to copare it to our training data. If the heatmap is too bright then the image is very different from our training data and the model will likely not work. We will prevent an image from being processed if it is not similar enough to our training data in order to prevent errors in predictions.
Predictive image regions: The brighter each pixel is in the heatmap the more influence it can have on the predictions. If the color is bright it means that a change in these pixels will change the prediction.
Disease Predictions: A probability indicating how likely the image contains the disease. 50% means the network is not sure... well, I'm not sure too lol
